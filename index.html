WeatherPy Webpage

As a follow-on to the WeatherPy and VacationPy assignments, this project summarizes the findings of those exercises on a webpage.

The plots contained on this website represent more than 500 cities around the world and includes meteorlogical data related to temperature, cloud cover, windspeed, and humidity. These factors are analyzed in scatter plots that compare them to a given city's latitude and determine any potential correlation.
